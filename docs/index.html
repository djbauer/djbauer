What I do

I mostly work with students in practical degree programs (e.g., nursing, education, social work, counseling, and psychology) who have little background experience with research and statistics. I understand the unique challenges that these students encounter when tasked with completing a research project, and use my expertise in the research process, mentorship, and project management to helps students overcome hurdles and complete their project efficiently and confidently. I have over 25 years of experience conducting research, teaching undergraduate and graduate research courses, and mentoring students through independent research projects.
